Guru Nanak Dev Ji

The founder of Sikhism

Here's a time line of Guru Nanak Dev Ji's Life:

  • 1469 - born in Rai Bhoi Ki Talvandi (present day Punjab, Pakistan)
  • 1476- enrolled at the village school
  • 1487 - marries Sulakhani in Batala
  • 1494 - first son, Sri Chand, was born
  • 1496 - left his family to travel in spiritual pursuits, called "Udasis"
  • 1497 - second son, Lakhmi Chand, was born
  • 1524 - moved to Kartarpur
  • 1539 - dies in Kartarpur (present day Pakistan) at the age of 70
